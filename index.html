Introduction to Computer Science

CS1201, Ecole Centrale School of Engineering, Mahindra University

About this Course

  1. What does computation mean, and how does it work?
  2. What does it mean to solve a problem through computer programming?
By answering questions like these, we aim to address the underlying principles behind various computer applications that we interact with in our everyday life. We subscribe to the philosophy that there is only one purpose to learn computer programming and that is to solve problems, and it is only through problem solving that we learn meaningful skills to work with computers. This is an introductory course and hence no prior knowledge on computers is expected. However, you will need to jog your common sense a bit to get hold of the subject. A general attitude and affinity to connect ideas and relate them to everyday experience with computers and smart devices will take you a long way.
